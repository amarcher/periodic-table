## Why This Concept
There is no viable video concept for astatine. It is arguably the rarest naturally occurring element on Earth, and its extreme radioactivity (longest-lived isotope has a half-life of only 8.1 hours) means it self-destructs before any visible sample could be accumulated. Any CGI or artistic interpretation would not be scientifically accurate.

## Interesting Properties for App Display
- **Density:** Estimated ~6.4 g/cm3 (never directly measured on a bulk sample)
- **Reactivity:** Assumed to behave like a heavier version of iodine, but less reactive; halogen behavior predicted
- **Melting/Boiling Point:** Estimated melting point ~302°C, boiling point ~337°C (never directly measured)
- **Appearance:** Never observed in bulk — predicted to be a dark, metallic-looking solid (possibly black) if it could exist in visible quantities
- **Other:** The rarest naturally occurring element — only about 70 milligrams exist in Earth's entire crust at any moment
